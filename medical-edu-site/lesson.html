Body Systems Overview

The human body is a complex and fascinating system made up of multiple interconnected systems working together to maintain life. In this lesson, we'll explore the major body systems and their primary functions.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the basic organization of the human body
  • Identify the major body systems and their components
  • Describe how different body systems work together
